# 2. The Retina Paradox


## Observation


The retina is one of the most sophisticated tissues in the human body.


It contains:

- photoreceptors
- neurons
- vascular networks
- signaling systems


But most people do not know where it is.


---

## Comedy Mechanism

Importance vs awareness gap.


---

## Premise


> The retina is one of the most important parts of human life.

> Unfortunately, most people only learn about it when it starts causing problems.
