# 1. Purpose

This file collects comedy materials around:

- computer vision

# 2. Human Eye vs AI Camera


## Observation


Human vision:

- biological
- emotional
- adaptive
- limited


AI vision:

- computational
- scalable
- data-driven


---

## Comedy Mechanism

Biology vs technology.


---

## Premise


> Humans spent millions of years developing eyes.

> AI spent several years learning images.

> The embarrassing part:

> AI may already recognize things humans miss.


---

# 3. The First Artificial Intelligence System


## Observation


The human visual system processes enormous information.


The retina itself performs complex computation.


---

## Comedy Mechanism

Humans underestimate biology.


---

## Premise


> The first AI system was already inside our eyes.

> We just didn't call it AI because we didn't invent the name yet.


---

# 4. Eye as Biological Computer


## Observation


The retina:

- detects signals
- processes information
- communicates with the brain


---

## Premise


> The retina is a biological computer.

> The difference is:

> It does not need software updates.

> It just slowly needs medical maintenance.

# 5. AI Can See, Humans Can Misunderstand


## Observation


AI can:

- recognize faces
- classify images
- analyze medical images


Humans can:

- misunderstand situations
- misjudge people
- ignore evidence


---

## Comedy Mechanism

Capability reversal.


---

## Premise


> AI's problem is that it does not understand humans.

> Humans' problem is that they understand each other too quickly.

# 6. Computer Vision


## Observation


Computer vision teaches machines to interpret images.


---

## Conflict

"Seeing is natural."


AI:

"Seeing is a difficult engineering problem."


---

## Premise


> Humans spent thousands of years thinking vision was easy.

> Then tried to teach computers and discovered:

> We had no idea how we do it.
