# 2. The Human Eye Paradox


## Observation

Humans evolved sophisticated visual systems.

The eye can:

- detect light
- recognize faces
- understand environments


But modern humans mostly use it for:

- smartphone screens
- emails
- spreadsheets
- social media


---

## Comedy Mechanism

Evolution vs modern lifestyle.


---

## Premise


> Humans spent millions of years developing eyesight.

> Then invented smartphones to use only the central 10 centimeters of their vision.

# 4. Myopia Culture


## Observation

In many Asian cultures:

Childhood:

Myopia can be associated with studying hard.


Later:

Myopia becomes a health concern.


---

## Comedy Mechanism

Success signal becomes health problem.


---

## Premise


> For many students:

> Getting glasses was proof you studied hard.

> Later you realize:

> You achieved success by losing the ability to see far away.

# 5. Looking Far vs Looking Near


## Observation


Young people:

Can look far.

Need to plan the future.


Older people:

Need to look close.

Need to read menus.


---

## Comedy Mechanism

Life reversal.


---

## Premise


> When you are young, you worry about seeing the future.

> When you are old, you worry about seeing today's lunch menu.
