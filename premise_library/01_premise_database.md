# 1. Purpose


A premise is the fundamental idea behind a comedy bit.


Structure:


Observation

↓

Unexpected connection

↓

Contradiction

↓

Premise


Example:


Observation:

Humans evolved excellent eyesight.


Contradiction:

Humans use it mostly for phones.


Premise:

> Humanity spent millions of years developing eyes, only to use them staring at a rectangle.

# 3. Vision Premise Library


---

## VP001

### Title

The Problem With Vision


### Observation

Everyone talks about vision.


Doctors.

Entrepreneurs.

Leaders.


### Contradiction

Everyone means something different.


### Premise


> Doctors want to restore your vision.

> Entrepreneurs want you to believe their vision.

> Investors want to know if your vision has value.


---

## VP002

### Title

Seeing Is Not Understanding


### Observation

Humans trust what they see.


### Contradiction

Seeing can be misleading.


### Premise


> The eye is the most trusted organ.

> Which is why it is so dangerous when it is wrong.


---

## VP003

### Title

The Human Blind Spot


### Observation

People fear blindness.


### Contradiction

Many mistakes happen when people are confident they see clearly.


### Premise


> The biggest human blind spot is believing we have no blind spots.
